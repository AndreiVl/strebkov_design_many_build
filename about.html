About us

We’re Strebkov Design (nice to meet you!) and we make great digital products for fintech companies. We specialize in helping startups get started the right way with UX/UI design that makes your apps work brilliantly for humans.

At Strebkov Design we’re experts at elevating fintech brands with apps and online products that your customers will need and want, and part of the reason why we’re so good at what we do is because of our 3-phase process. We’ve spent 10 years shaping it and now we use it on every project. It keeps us on track so we can deliver a better product to you in less time.

Here’s how it works:

Phase 1. Discovery and Strategy

You wouldn’t try and design a glove if you’d never seen a hand before, which is why we won’t design a user interface before we’ve researched who’s going to be using it and how they’ll interact with it. With that in mind, we start by asking questions. We aim to understand your business, your customers, your product, and your market. We build a clear picture of your whole ecosystem so that we know where your app fits within it.

We research your business requirements, audit the product, and conduct market research so we understand both your customers and your competitors. We explore the product strategy, we define your users with empathy maps and personas, and we put their needs in context with customer journey maps and storyboards. We look at any solutions that you or your competitors already offer, and we frame the problem using POV statements.

Phase 2. Design and Delivery

Phase 2 begins with product definition and prototyping. We create user flows based on user scenarios and we map informational architecture. We use wireframing and interactive prototyping to hone our designs further.

It’s at this time that we also think about your product identity and visual language, exploring product definition and prototyping. We create user flows based on user scenarios and then dive into information architecture and wireframing. From these inquiries we define the brand’s visual identity and formalise it with high fidelity mockups, an interactive prototype, a UI kit and patterns libraries, specs and metrics. Finally, we deliver all the finished visual assets like graphics and icons, so your developers can create the product.

Phase 3. Development Support

We want everything to go smoothly, even after we’ve delivered, so we’ll give you 45 to 60 days of development support (depending on which package you selected) to iron out any creases. This includes regular UX/UI reviews of the layouts our designers built, answers to your questions, and design fixes (within the initial scope of the project) should they be needed.

Why you should work with us

We’re a group of skilled and passionate practitioners who put ourselves in your users’ shoes to understand and enhance their journey. We deliver elegant and functional visual interfaces for fintech startups, wherever they may be in the world, and we owe our success to being fast, effective and able to slot right into your startup team.
