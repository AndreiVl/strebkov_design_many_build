Phase 1. Discovery and Strategy

You wouldn’t try and design a glove if you’d never seen a hand before, which is why we won’t design a user interface before we’ve researched who’s going to be using it and how they’ll interact with it. With that in mind, we start by asking questions. We aim to understand your business, your customers, your product, and your market. We build a clear picture of your whole ecosystem so that we know where your app fits within it.

We research your business requirements, audit the product, and conduct market research so we understand both your customers and your competitors. We explore the product strategy, we define your users with empathy maps and personas, and we put their needs in context with customer journey maps and storyboards. We look at any solutions that you or your competitors already offer, and we frame the problem using POV statements.
